About Me

My Journey

With over 15 years of dedicated practice and teaching, I have guided hundreds of individuals on their path to inner peace and wellbeing. My journey began during my own struggles with anxiety, when I discovered the transformative power of breathwork and mindfulness.

After completing rigorous training in India and Thailand, I returned with a mission to make these ancient practices accessible to modern lives. I blend traditional techniques with contemporary science to create personalized programs that address the unique needs of each student.

My approach is rooted in compassion, patience, and the belief that everyone can benefit from mindful awareness. Whether you're seeking stress relief, emotional balance, or spiritual growth, I provide a supportive space for your transformation.

Beyond one-on-one sessions, I regularly conduct workshops for corporations, schools, and healthcare facilities, bringing the benefits of meditation to diverse communities. My work has been featured in several wellness publications, and I continue to deepen my knowledge through ongoing study and practice.

Credentials

  • Certified Meditation Teacher (600-hour program)
  • Advanced Breathwork Facilitator
  • Yoga Alliance RYT-500
  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR) Instructor
  • Continuing education in trauma-sensitive approaches
